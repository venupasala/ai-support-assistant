# Refund & Returns Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not, we offer a straightforward refund and return process.

## Refund Window
Refunds are allowed within **7 days** of the purchase date. After 7 days, we cannot offer a full refund.

## Eligibility
To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused and in the same condition you received it.
- It must be in the original packaging.
- A receipt or proof of purchase is required.

## How to Request a Refund
1. Email support@company.com with your order number.
2. Describe the reason for the return.
3. Our team will respond within 24 hours with return instructions.
4. Ship the item back using the provided return label.
5. Once received and inspected, a refund will be processed within **3-5 business days**.

## Non-Refundable Items
- Digital downloads
- Gift cards
- Sale items (marked as final sale)

## Exchanges
We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. To request an exchange, email support@company.com.

## Late or Missing Refunds
If you haven't received a refund within 5 business days:
1. Check your bank account again.
2. Contact your credit card company.
3. Contact us at support@company.com.
